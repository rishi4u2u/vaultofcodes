Chocolate Cake

Prep: 20m Cook: 30m Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup hot coffee or water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch pan.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, salt.
  3. Add egg, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in hot coffee (or water) until batter is thin and glossy.
  5. Pour into pan; bake 28–32 minutes. Cool completely.
  6. Frost as desired. Slice and enjoy!
